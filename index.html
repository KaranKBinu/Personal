How to Calculate CGPA - Step by Step Guide

Created by Karan K Binu

For any questions or comments, contact me at Gmail

  1. Step 1: Start by entering the number of subjects you want to calculate your CGPA for in the "Number of Subjects" field.

  2. Step 2: Click the "Enter" button to generate input fields for each subject based on the number you entered.

  3. Step 3: For each subject, enter the following information:

    • Credit: Enter the credit value for the subject in the provided input field.
    • Grade: Select the grade you received for the subject from the dropdown list.

    Repeat this step for all subjects you want to include in the calculation.

  4. Step 4: After entering the details for all subjects, click the "Calculate CGPA" button.

  5. Step 5: The calculated CGPA and percentage will be displayed in a modal window.

  6. Step 6: You can choose to clear the input fields by clicking the "Clear" button for a fresh calculation, or make adjustments and recalculate your CGPA as needed.

That's it! You've successfully calculated your CGPA using the CGPA Calculator.
