How to Calculate CGPA - Step by Step Guide

Created by Karan K Binu

For any questions or comments, contact me at Gmail

  1. NOTE: if you do not have a subject then leave it as like it is

    Step 1: Begin by selecting your department and semester from the dropdown lists.

  2. Step 2: Click the "Enter" button to generate input fields for your selected department and semester. Subjects will be automatically populated based on your selection.

  3. Step 3: For each subject, enter the following information:

    • Credit: Ensure the credit value for the subject is valid (a positive number) in the provided field.
    • Grade: Choose the grade you received for the subject from the dropdown list.

    Repeat this step for all subjects you want to include in the calculation.

  4. Step 4: Once you have entered the details for all subjects, click the "Calculate CGPA" button.

  5. Step 5: The calculated CGPA and percentage will be displayed in a modal window.

  6. Step 6: You can opt to clear the input fields by clicking the "Clear" button for a fresh calculation, or make adjustments and recalculate your CGPA as needed.

That's it! You've successfully calculated your CGPA using the CGPA Calculator.
